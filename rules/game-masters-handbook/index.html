Introduction

This guide will walk you through the concepts of how the game world works and what your role as the Game Master entails. As Game Master (GM), your role is architect, narrator, decider, intermediary, friend, enemy, and anything else that is required for the players to navigate the game world. It’s a big responsibility, but it’s also a lot of fun!
