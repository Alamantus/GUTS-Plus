Definitions

Monday, January 1, 0001

Definition of Game Master

The Game Master (GM for short) is the narrator and controller of the game world.

Monday, January 1, 0001

Definition of In-Character

Actions taken by a character rather than the player.

Monday, January 1, 0001

Definition of Non-Playable Character

A character played and controlled by the Game Master instead of by the players.

Monday, January 1, 0001

Definition of Out-of-Character

Actions and dialog taken by the player but not the character.

Monday, January 1, 0001

Definition of nDs

The formula representing how many of what type of die to roll (n dice with s sides). For example, 2D6 means a roll of 2 six-sided dice.
